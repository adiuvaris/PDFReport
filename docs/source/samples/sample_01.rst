
Sample 01
=========

There is just an instance of a *Report* and all attributes get a default value.
This means that the page format A4 in portrait mode. The margins are 20mm on the left side and 10mm on all other sides.

To show the printable area a BoxFrame is added to the report body.
The usage of *BoxFrames* can be found in a later examples.
